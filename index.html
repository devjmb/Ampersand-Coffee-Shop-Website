Coffee & Who We Are.

At Ampersand, we believe coffee is more than just a drink, it’s a fuel for focus, ideas, & meaningful connections. Founded and managed by Ranz Cutarra & Julian Buizon, Ampersand is Manila’s productivity café built for students, professionals, & creators. With work-friendly spaces, collab corners, & excellent brews, we’re here to make every cup a step toward progress.
